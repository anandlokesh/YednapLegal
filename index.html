About Yednap Legal

Yednap Legal, a specialized intellectual property law firm, is dedicated towards protection and enforcements of all forms of intellectual property of its clients. Our practice areas range from prosecution, enforcement, consultation, commercialization and licensing and such other related transactions of Intellectual Property Rights, namely, Patents, Trade Marks, Designs, Copyrights, IC Layouts and Geographical Indications. Roster of our clients range from proprietors and start-ups to Multi-National Corporates and Government Institutions. Expert Consultants and skilled attorneys work in collaboration to provide expert opinions and services regarding all intellectual property related matters. At Yednap legal, we aim at understanding the requirements of our clients and providing effective and efficient legal and consultancy services to safeguard our clients rights and interests.



INNOVATION and the need to convert IDEAS into WORK

Fierce global competition and ever-increasing demand for rapid growth and improvement in technology and quality of goods and services across all major industries and sectors have increased the need for innovation in the market in order to stand apart and develop a business. Research and Development in today’s era is growing every day and entities have become increasingly vigilant regarding protection of such innovation through acquiring Intellectual Property Rights on their innovated concepts. In such competitive scenario, one must focus on creating, developing and innovating in the sector or industry in which one is indulged. We at Yednap Legal focus on realization and identification of such Intellectual Property of our clients and ensuring grant of protection of such precious rights and if needed enforcing such rights against potential infringers.



IP COMMERCIALIZATION, VALUATION and TRANSACTIONS

The process of innovation and development is not always carried out in big R& D cells of hugely funded industries with state of the art infrastructure. Scientists, developers and university researchers also work solely towards their innovative projects to create a novel and industrially invention. Similarly, sole proprietors working for years under the same brand name create an immense amount of goodwill and reputation in the local market. However, the hindrance in the above cases, commonly faced, is the lack of monetary funds to commercialize the Intellectual Property in order for it to reach a larger customer base or for a greater exploitation and application of one’s inventions. At Yednap Legal, we assist our clients by soliciting for them and by becoming the middlemen between them and the interested clients ready to invest in their projects either by acquiring the ownership or by providing funds for research, development and application of the same. We ensure safeguarding of interests of both the parties and mutual benefit of both sides to establish a win-win situation.


While entering such arrangements and signing agreements, a major issue arises regarding the valuation of the subject Intellectual Property of the arrangement. Yednap Legal, through expert affiliates, ensures proper valuation of the intellectual property after carefully considering all commercial aspects of the transactions to be taken place. For entities or individuals not willing to transfer the ownership of the IP, Yednap Legal explores licensing and other related transactional opportunities such as franchising, brand holding etc.
